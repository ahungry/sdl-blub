A big pack of the colourful RPG characters that will be a perfect match for
16x16 tiles.

This set includes:

10 recognisable RPG heroes (fighters, healers, mages and rangers)
7 non-human species (flower, grass spirit, mermaid, mushroom, poprigunchik,
seed, shrump)
36 NPCs (aristocrates, bard, clown, cultist, dancer, king, prince and
princess, person in carnival mask, person in winter clothes, townfolk of
different ages)
If you use this in your game, please let me play it!
